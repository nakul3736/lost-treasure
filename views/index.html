Instructions

  • In this game we have to find the treasure on the map by clicking at different locations.
  • So the main objective of the game is to find the treasure in minimum number of clicks.
  • On every click hint will be given to you in terms of temprature.
  • Colder the temprature farther you will be from the treasure and if hotter the tempreature means you are closer to treaure.
  • Terms to indicate temprature in their ascending order are :-
  • Freezing < Really Cold < Cold < Warm < Hot < Really Hot < Boiling Hot
